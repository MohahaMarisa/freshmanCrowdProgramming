|| ABOUT

The heart of Design is in serving people. I want to do human centered design at the forefront of the sociotechnical landscape. In working with emerging technologies, I want to create delightful interactions that make more technology feel like less, and that ultimately focus on empowering us to do more and live fully.

In an era of rapid changes, it takes someone able to wear a lot of hats. I have quite the 'hat' collection, and a life of curiosity has made me incredibly good at rapidly acquiring more; however many it takes to enable the design.

|| GOALS

Wake up early. Do good. Work smart. Create products that resonate on an emotional level. Make insightful, meaningful, aesthetic visualizations. Brighten someone's day. Write a thank you note.
...
And possibly sleep more.
